Ohio Malayalee Christian Congregation

About Us

Founded on the principles of unity, diversity, and shared faith, the Ohio Malayalee Christian Congregation (OMCC) stands as an ecumenical beacon within the heart of Central Ohio, comprising members from various Kerala churches including the Church of South India (CSI), Jacobite Syrian Christian Church, and Mar Thoma Syrian Church. Gathering together from cities such as Columbus, Dayton, and Cincinnati, OMCC holds worship services once a month, offering a unique blend of traditional Kerala liturgy and contemporary worship elements, drawing individuals and families from across the region to worship, fellowship, and grow together in their faith.

In addition to monthly worship services, OMCC is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of its members through regular Sunday School sessions, providing children and youth with opportunities to engage with Scripture and deepen their understanding of the faith. Moreover, OMCC holds a monthly prayer meeting, offering a sacred space for communal supplication and intercession. As an ecumenical congregation, OMCC embodies the diversity of the Body of Christ, welcoming individuals united in their commitment to following Christ and living out His teachings, striving to glorify God and advance His kingdom here on earth through shared worship, education, and prayer.
